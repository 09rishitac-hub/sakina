Our Story

Born From Real Feelings

Sakina Initiative started because we knew how lonely it felt to struggle in silence. We built the space we wished we had.

Too many teens are going through hard things completely alone — anxiety, loneliness, family stress, identity questions — and feeling like no one around them truly gets it.

We're not therapists, not adults with textbooks — we're teens who've been there and want to walk alongside you. The name Sakina means peace, tranquility, and calm.

Guides & Helplines

Tools to Help You Cope 🌱

Practical, honest frameworks gathered by our team to guide you through tough moments.

🌬️

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4s, hold for 4s, exhale for 4s, hold for 4s. Calms your nervous system instantly.

🌿

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 sights, 4 touches, 3 sounds, 2 smells, and 1 taste to pull yourself out of a mind spiral.

✍️

Journaling Thoughts

Write "Right now I feel..." uninterrupted for 5 minutes. No filters. Delete or burn it after if needed.

🚫

Setting Boundaries

"No" is a complete sentence. Protecting your energy isn't selfish, it's necessary health.
